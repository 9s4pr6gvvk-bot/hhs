Welcome

Tucked away. Thoughtfully set up.

Harrogate Hidden Stay sits behind a garden gate in the Duchy — one of Harrogate's most sought-after residential streets — and that's rather the point. It's private, it's quiet, and it's yours.

Inside, the apartment is set up the way you'd want your own place to feel: a log burner to settle beside, a kitchen that's properly equipped, patio doors that open onto a private garden with outdoor seating and a BBQ.

"Feels more like a home than a rental, with quality fixtures and fittings."

Whether it's a spa‑town weekend, a longer work trip, or a base for exploring the Yorkshire Dales, this is somewhere you'll actually look forward to coming back to at the end of the day.

What's inside

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

The apartment sleeps up to four across two living spaces — ideal for couples, small families, or a working stay with room to spread out.

King Bedroom

A spacious room with a king size bed, dressed for a proper night's sleep — the kind you came away for.

Lounge & Log Burner

Patio doors to the garden, a TV, and a log burner for evenings when you just want to stay in.

Second Living Room

A sofa bed and desk behind bi‑fold doors — a second bedroom by night, a quiet office by day.

Dining Kitchen

Fully equipped with a range of appliances — ready for a proper home‑cooked meal or a lazy morning coffee.

Private Garden

Outdoor seating, a BBQ, and your own green space — right through the patio doors.

Off‑Road Parking

Your own private parking space, right outside the apartment — no meters, no hunting for a spot.
